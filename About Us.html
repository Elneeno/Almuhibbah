About Us

Al- Muhibbah Institute Of ICT and Entreprenuership was Established under the NGO of Her Excellency the First Lady of Bauchi state (Dr.) Aisha Bala Muhammed, Al- Muhibbah Foundation. The institute was established to promote digital literacy and entreprenuership skills relevant to the advancement of Nigeria and global economy.

The Institute is Registered with Corporate Affairs Commision and is Accredited by National Board for Technical Education (NBTE), Center for Management Development (CMD) and Central Bank of Nigeria (Nirsal -AGSMEIS Entrepreneurship Development Institute). It is also a CERTIPORT- Authorized Training & Testing Center.

The Institute also has membership of the Global Digital Literacy Council, Nigerian Society of Engineers and Nigerian Computer Society.

Some of the Institute key programs Include:

  • CERTIPORT- IC3 Global Standard 5 Digital literacy Training & Certification.
  • Cisco Digital literacy
  • Management Informatics
  • Network and system security
  • Computer Software Engineering
  • Computer Hardware engineering
  • Network and system security
  • Telecommunication Technology
  • Multimedia Technology
  • Solar, Electrical installation & GSM repairs
  • Small Business Management
  • Cyber security
