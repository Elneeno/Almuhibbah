About Al- Muhibbah Institute Of ICT And Entreprenuership

Mission And Vision

Mission

To promote digital literacy and entrepreneurship skills relevant to the advancement of Nigeria and global economy.

Vision

To be the leading provider of modern technology and business skills by utilising local content and materials to support Nigeria's drive for self sufficiency in human and Technological development.

Goals

  • To provide world class teaching and training facilities in the areas of Information Communication Technology and Entrepreneurship development.
  • To serve as research and demonstration center for emerging technologies.
  • To establish educational, research and business collaborations with national & International institutions.
  • To carry out consultancy services in the areas of ICT & digital economy.
  • To establish, operate and maintain skill acquisition centres
  • To create employment opportunities.

About Us

Al- Muhibbah Institute Of ICT and Entreprenuership was Established under the philantrophic NGO of Her Excellency the First Lady of Bauchi state (Dr.) Aisha Bala Muhammed, Al- Muhibbah Foundation. The institute was established to promote digital literacy and entreprenuership skills relevant to the advancement of Nigeria and global economy.
